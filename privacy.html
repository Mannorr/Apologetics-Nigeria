Privacy Policy

Privacy

Privacy policy.

This policy explains what information Apologetics Nigeria collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. We keep it simple, because our approach is simple: we only collect what you choose to give us, and we never sell it.

What we collect

When you submit our contact form or subscribe to our newsletter, we collect the details you enter — your name, email address, and any message or organisation you provide. We do not collect this information unless you choose to send it.

How we use it

We use your details only to respond to your enquiry and, if you subscribe, to send occasional ministry updates — new videos, resources, and news. We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for their marketing.

How it is processed

Our forms are handled by Formspree, a form-processing service that delivers your submissions to us securely. Your data is transmitted to us through their platform and stored so we can respond and, where relevant, keep you informed.

Your choices

You can unsubscribe from our emails at any time. You may also ask us to correct or delete the information we hold about you — just get in touch and we will act on your request promptly.

Contact

For any question about your data or this policy, email hello@apologeticsnigeria.org.

This policy may be updated from time to time. Last updated: 2026.
